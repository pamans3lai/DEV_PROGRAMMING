Always remeber that the goal of statistics is not to perform numerous calculations using the formulae, but to gain understanding of your data. The calculations can be done by the calculator or computer. The understanding must come from you. If you can thoroughly grasp the basics of statistics, you can be more confident in the decisions you make in life.
